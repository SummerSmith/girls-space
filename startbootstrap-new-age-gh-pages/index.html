What Makes Us Unique?

Studies show that of undergraduate computer science majors, only about 20% are female. We want to help close the gender-gap in computer science by engaging girls in our game. Debbie Sterling, CEO of GoldieBlox, gave a talk at Grace Hopper Convention 2017 where she emphasized the differences between games designed for girls and boys. While boys like to build, girls prefer to build stories and choose activities that allow for a more creative experience. With this in mind, we designed our game to be an interactive multi-level quest that the player can go on, complete with challenges that teach programming concepts. It can be intimidating to learn programming- trust us, we've been there. We want everyone to enjoy our game, but hope that our secret approach to learning programming concepts will engage more girls in the field. Play our game, and you too can run the world.
